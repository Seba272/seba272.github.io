Sebastiano Nicolussi Golo

I am a Senior Researcher at the Department of Mathematics of the University of Fribourg, in the Canton of Fribourg (in Switzerland), which is outside the European Union, but still on planet Earth.

If you want to contact me, you can write me an email to sebastiano2.72@gmail.com.

My main areas of research are:

  • Metric Geometry,
  • Geometric Measure Theory,
  • Lie Group Theory,
  • Sub-Riemannian Geometry,
  • Analysis on Metric Spaces,
  • Harmonic Analysis.

Short Bio

I obtained a double PhD degree in 2017 from the University of Jyväskylä (Finland, EU) and from the University of Trento (Italy, EU), under the supervision of Enrico Le Donne and Francesco Serra Cassano. I later worked in Birmingham (UK) with Alessio Martini for one year, and in Padova (IT, EU) with Davide Vittone for one year and a half. From 2020 to 2023, I have been a postdoc at the Department of Mathematics and Statistics of the University of Jyväskylä in Finland (EU), under the supervision of Katrin Fässler and of Enrico Le Donne.

Then, I took a break in the academic year 2023–2024, when I lived in the Zen monastery Fudenji.

As of 2024, I am working in the academy, under the supervision of my boss, mentor and friend Enrico Le Donne.
